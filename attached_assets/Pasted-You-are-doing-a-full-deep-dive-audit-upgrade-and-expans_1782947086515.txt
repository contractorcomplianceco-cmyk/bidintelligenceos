IMPORTANT PRODUCT RELATIONSHIP
VoiceConnect integrates with BidIntelligenceOS. VoiceConnect is not the client and not the main product. It is a field-capture/input layer that syncs site walkthrough notes, voice notes, field photos, risks, questions, and daily updates into BidIntelligenceOS.

BidIntelligenceOS is the main platform.

NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
- Do not expose internal CCA pricing formulas.
- Do not show internal margin logic, COGS, bid confidence, competitor notes, or AI strategy inside client-facing bid packages.
- Do not promise guaranteed bid wins.
- Do not make competitor intelligence look like illegal spying or private data access.
- Competitor information must be framed as lawful, public, contractor-provided, historical, or market-observed data only.
- All AI output must be reviewable before client-facing export.
- The app must feel like an operating system, not a simple bid form.
